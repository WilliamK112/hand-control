📚 School Technology System Requirements

Core Priorities

The new system should focus on reliability, strong Wi-Fi, scalability, and equal access. Reliability is important because teachers and students will use technology every day, so the network must work without frequent outages. Strong wireless coverage is necessary since every student will have a laptop and must be able to connect in classrooms and across campus. Scalability matters because the school must support many devices at once and still leave room for future growth. Equal access is also important, since all students need the same opportunity to use school technology for learning.

Security & Management

Security and device management are also essential features. The school must protect student and staff data with secure logins, firewalls, filtering, and backups. Centralized management helps the IT staff update software, monitor devices, and fix problems more efficiently.

Requirements Checklist

  • Secure login authentication
  • Firewall protection
  • Content filtering
  • Automated backups
  • Centralized device management
  • Software update system
  • Remote monitoring capabilities

Classroom Support

The laptops and network should also support classroom needs such as online assignments, testing, video calls, and digital learning tools. These features are important because the goal is not just to replace old equipment, but to create a safe, dependable, and effective learning environment.

✨ Goal: Create a safe, dependable, and effective learning environment for all students and staff.
